Kyle_J_Young - Systems Administrator, Consultant, Homelabber, Tinkerer

DESCRIPTION

Kyle has been a consultant for multiple state government unemployment divisions, designing and implementing automated correspondence systems. He has also served as a Linux systems administrator for the unemployment production systems of two states, handling deployments, system monitoring, troubleshooting and root cause analysis.

During Kyle's time as a consultant, he led his team in requirements gathering, design, and implementation of a new correspondence automation system to replace existing mainframe infrastructure, and provided training to multiple client teams to maintain and administer the solution.

As a systems administrator, Kyle has experience with clustered hypervisors (Proxmox), Storage systems configuration and management (ZFS, LVM), database administration, and configuration automation utilizing Ansible. He is familiar with log monitoring, deployment of containers and VMs, and hardware troubleshooting.

Network administration has been a core component of his history, including installation and configuration of firewalls, switches, and physical fiber and wiring. He is familiar with Cisco, Arista, Ruckus, and Dell console configuration.
